PRODUCT QUALITY REPORT — USB-C Hub (SKU-UCH500)

Engineering has confirmed a firmware defect in UCH500 units shipped in May 2026.
Issue: Overheating due to inadequate power regulation in the USB-A ports.
Compatibility issue: Certain MacBook models (M2/M3) do not recognise the hub.
A firmware patch is being developed. ETA: 2026-06-20.
Customers should be offered a refund or replacement with the updated model.
Units with complaint reports of overheating should be recalled.
